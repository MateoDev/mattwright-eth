*Bonjour la communauté!* Quorum has bounties for ETHParis.

We had a great time at ETHDenver and are bringing the same energy to Paris. The Quorum team is on a mission to connect with developers building privacy, performance, and public/private integrations on Quorum's permissioned Go Ethereum.

## The Bounties

Three prizes, $1,000 each:

**Privacy** — Best use of Quorum's privacy features

**High Performance** — Best use of Quorum's fast transaction throughput

**Public/Private Chain** — Best use of tools and protocols from public Ethereum on Quorum (MetaMask, Truffle, 0x, Dharma Protocol, etc.)

## Build Ideas

A few directions to consider:

- Extend MetaMask to support Quorum private transaction signing through a hardware wallet (Trezor, Ledger)
- Build a bridge between Quorum and public Ethereum, allowing asset transfer or cross-chain references
- Build a governance DApp for adding/removing parties from a Quorum network

Want to suggest something? Find me on Slack: `@Matt_Quorum`

## What Was Built at ETHDenver

**Tidbits** — A web3 game with no extensions, no modals. Users assemble poems from word magnets. The contract manages gas for users automatically — they never touch ETH directly.

**RToken** — A permissioned security token standard for globally compliant ERC20 issuance, leveraging Quorum for investor privacy and institutional-grade compliance.

**GeoHunter** — A treasure hunt DApp where QR codes must be scanned in order. Each QR code has an associated IPFS image showing its location, with all user and tag data stored on-chain.

Come find us at ETHParis — we'll be at the Quorum booth all weekend.
