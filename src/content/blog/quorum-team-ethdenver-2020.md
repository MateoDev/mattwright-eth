This isn't our first rodeo in Colorado — but it is our second. The Quorum team is excited to reunite with the Ethereum ecosystem at ETHDenver 2020 for a weekend of building.

If you were at ETHDenver last year, you may have stopped by our booth. If you didn't catch us — now is the time.

## Why Build with Quorum?

Quorum is a fork of the Go Ethereum client (geth) — enterprise-ready, open-source, built to address the specific challenges of blockchain adoption in an enterprise environment: privacy, speed, throughput.

**Primary features:**
- Transaction and contract privacy
- Multiple voting-based consensus mechanisms
- Network/peer permissions management
- Higher performance than public Ethereum

**Core components:**
- Quorum Node (modified Geth client)
- Transaction Manager (Tessera)
- Encryption Enclave

Real-world use cases already in production: video game royalties, healthcare data management, tokenization of gold bars, interbank settlement. [See the full list of 50+ projects →](/50-projects-building-on-quorum)

## What We're Looking For

We want to see tools, APIs, and DApps built on Quorum. The bounties:

- **Privacy** — best use of Quorum's privacy features
- **Performance** — best use of Quorum's transaction throughput
- **Public/Private Bridge** — best use of public Ethereum tools on Quorum (MetaMask, Truffle, 0x, etc.)

## What Was Built Last Year

From ETHDenver 2019: a seamless web3 gaming experience with no browser extensions and no modals, a permissioned security token standard for global ERC20 issuance compliant with investor-level regulation, and a treasure hunt DApp where QR codes stored on IPFS track user progress on-chain.

Come find us at the Quorum booth. We'll be heads down with builders all weekend.
